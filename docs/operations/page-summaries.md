## Design Principles

### All Pages
- **Color Scheme:** Moss green (#5A7A5A) primary
- **Typography:** Large, readable (18px+ body text)
- **Accessibility:** High contrast, WCAG AA compliant
- **Mobile:** Responsive, touch-friendly
- **Simplicity:** Clean, uncluttered, book-focused

### Target Audience
- **Age:** 45-75 years (primary: 55-70)
- **Reading Habits:** Literary enthusiasts, Scottish culture
- **Technical Level:** Non-technical users (David & Kirsty)
- **Accessibility:** Older readers, clear navigation

---

## Content Strategy

### Tone of Voice
- Literary and thoughtful
- Authentic and genuine
- Welcoming and accessible
- Heritage-focused
- Passionate about books

1. "Publishing books we love, not just books that sell"

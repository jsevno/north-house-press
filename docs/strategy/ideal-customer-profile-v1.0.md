## ICP OVERVIEW

### What is an ICP?
An Ideal Customer Profile defines the perfect customer for North House Press - the type of reader who:
- Gets maximum value from our books
- Is easiest to reach and convert
- Has highest lifetime value (buys multiple books)
- Becomes an advocate and recommends us
- Aligns with our mission and values

### Why This Matters
As a small independent publisher with limited marketing budget, focusing on the right customers is critical. Our ICP helps us:
- Target marketing effectively
- Create relevant content
- Design the right customer experience
- Build a community of engaged readers
- Maximize word-of-mouth growth

## BUYING BEHAVIOR

### Decision Process

1. **Awareness**
   - Discovers through: email newsletter, social media, book reviews, word of mouth
   - Sees book cover or author name
   - Reads description and reviews

2. **Consideration**
   - Checks book details (author, description, price)
   - Reads sample pages if available
   - Looks for reviews or recommendations
   - Considers if it fits their interests

3. **Decision**
   - Price is reasonable (£12-£20 acceptable)
   - Book aligns with interests (Scottish, literary)
   - Trust in publisher (independent, quality)
   - Easy purchase process

4. **Post-Purchase**
   - Reads and evaluates
   - Shares if enjoyed
   - Considers buying more from publisher
   - Joins mailing list for updates

### Decision Timeline
- **Impulse:** 10% (if strong recommendation)
- **Days:** 40% (consider for a few days)
- **Weeks:** 40% (wait for right moment)
- **Months:** 10% (save for later)

### Decision Influencers
- **Personal Recommendations** - Highest influence
- **Book Reviews** - Trusted sources (Guardian, TLS, etc.)
- **Author Reputation** - Known authors (Neil Gunn)
- **Publisher Reputation** - Independent, quality-focused
- **Book Cover** - Important for discovery
- **Price** - Reasonable, not primary factor

### Purchase Triggers
- New release from favorite author
- Special edition (centenary, etc.)
- Recommendation from trusted source
- Gift occasion (birthday, Christmas)
- Book club selection
- Limited availability

### Objections & Concerns

**Common Objections:**
1. **"Is this worth the price?"**
   - Response: Quality independent publishing, direct from publisher
   - Solution: Sample pages, reviews, author reputation

2. **"Will I actually read it?"**
   - Response: Curated list, quality over quantity
   - Solution: Clear descriptions, reading guides

3. **"Can I trust the quality?"**
   - Response: Professional publishing, established authors
   - Solution: Reviews, author credentials, sample content

4. **"Shipping costs?"**
   - Response: UK/Ireland only, reasonable rates
   - Solution: Clear shipping information, bundle deals

## NEGATIVE PERSONAS (Who is NOT our customer)

### Exclude These Segments

1. **Price-Only Shoppers**

   - Doesn't value independent publishing
   - Won't pay £15-20 for quality books

   - Prefers YA, fantasy, romance genres
   - Not interested in Scottish literary fiction
   - Different reading preferences

3. **Academic Only**

   - Not general readership

## ICP-DRIVEN DECISIONS

### Product/Service Design
**Based on ICP, our offering should:**
- Focus on Scottish literature and culture
- Offer both print and ebook options
- Provide quality production (covers, typography)
- Include author background and context
- Create curated, not mass-market, list
- Build direct publisher-reader relationship

### Pricing Strategy
**Based on ICP, our pricing should:**
- **Print Books:** £12-£20 (acceptable range)
- **Ebooks:** £8-£12 (20-30% of print price)
- **Bundles:** Offer discounts for multiple books
- **Shipping:** Clear, reasonable UK/Ireland rates
- **Value:** Emphasize quality, not discount

### Marketing & Messaging
**Based on ICP, our marketing should:**
- **Channels:** Email, Facebook, Goodreads, book reviews
- **Tone:** Literary, authentic, welcoming, heritage-focused
- **Messages:** Scottish culture, quality literature, independent spirit
- **Content:** Author stories, book backgrounds, Scottish culture
- **Timing:** Monthly/quarterly (not overwhelming)

### Customer Experience
**Based on ICP, our experience should:**
- Easy website navigation (older audience)
- Large, readable text (accessibility)
- Clear book descriptions
- Personal, friendly service
- Direct communication (email preferred)
- Community building (newsletter, events)
